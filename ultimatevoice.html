Where Every Voice

Competition Structure

Three Rounds

⭐

Preliminary Round

Online Evaluation

Submit your audio recording online for review by our professional judging team — no travel required. Easy, accessible participation for everyone.

Free Entry
⭐

Semi-Final

In-Person Competition

Qualified contestants compete live at MRU Conservatory, receiving real-time feedback from professional mentors and connecting with fellow music enthusiasts.

$20 Registration Fee
⭐

Final

Bella Concert Hall

Finalists receive professional training in stage presence, attire, makeup, and vocal performance to shine in the finals and reach new heights of musical glory.
